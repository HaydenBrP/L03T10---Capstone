About

Registered on October 26 1984,  the Deadly Serious Party was a political party stood candidates in elections in the 1980s. The party was created by Australian candidates with the goal of mocking other candidates.  Its platform included dispatching a flock of killer penguins to protect Australia's coastline from Argentine invasion, an age freeze, and the appointment of silly people to all the portfolios that matter. It was deregistered effective 2 November 1988 for not having the required 500 members.

This year we're brining it back to plague South Africa!
